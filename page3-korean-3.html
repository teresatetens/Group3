Sundubu-jjigae

Spicy Soft Tofu Stew

This spicy dish is made by boiling soft dubu (tofu), beef, seafoods and vegetables in a meat broth in a stone pot; sometimes, eggs are used. The soup may be boiled clear, or it may be spiced with gochut-garu (red chili pepper powder). Dubu is one of the most popular ingredients in Korean dishes.
