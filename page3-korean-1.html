Janchi-guksu

Banquet Noodles

A soup made of thin noodles topped with various garnishing before a clear broth made from dried anchovy is poured over top for serving. It is a traditional Korean dish that was served to the guests at special parties including marriage ceremonies, birthdays and, particularly on the 60th birthday.
